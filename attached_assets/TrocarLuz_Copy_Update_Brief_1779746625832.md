## The USP that must come through on every page

Residential customers in Brazil **cannot switch energy suppliers alone**. Brazilian law requires them to use a certified retail energy broker (comercializador varejista) registered with CCEE. TrocarLuz and Ótima Energia are that broker. We do not just show you options — we are the legally required vehicle through which you make the switch.

This is not a comparison site that leaves you to figure it out. This is the platform that takes you all the way through.
